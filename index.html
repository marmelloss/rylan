About Rylan

Rylan is a social and dating application designed to help users discover meaningful connections through intelligent matching systems. It supports a smooth experience from onboarding to finding matches, with a focus on relevance and simplicity.

How Rylan Works

Rylan uses a variety of methods to support profile discovery and connection suggestions:

  • Matching Algorithms: Implements techniques such as Branch & Bound, Monte Carlo Simulation, PageRank, MST, and ART Clustering.
  • Image Processing: Incorporates tools like YOLOv8, DeepLabV3, and Apple Vision Framework to provide context-aware insights.
  • Radius Expansion: Adjusts search radius over time to help users connect within reasonable proximity.
  • Behavior Analysis: Looks at how users interact—such as time spent viewing and liking—to adjust future recommendations.

Tailored Experience

Rylan adapts to each user's interaction patterns. It uses probabilistic ranking, multi-field modeling, and examples to help present profiles that are relevant to individual interests.

Why It Matters

With countless profiles available, Rylan emphasizes relevance and ease of use. The design and interaction model aim to keep the experience focused, intuitive, and adaptable.
